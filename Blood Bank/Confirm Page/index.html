Before we go ahead with confirming your registration to be a user. There are some basic requirements you need to fulfill in order to become a blood donor. They are:

  • Age
    You must be between the age 18 to 65.
  • Weight
    You must weigh at least 50kg.
  • Health
    Donating is not permitted if you have a stomach illness, cold, flu, sore throat, cold sore, or any other ailment. You are not eligible to donate for 6 months following the date of a recent tattoo or body piercing.
